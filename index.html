About REAP 2026

The Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process (REAP) is the centralized counseling procedure conducted by the Centre for Electronic Governance (CEG), Jaipur. It serves as the primary channel for students aiming to secure admission into B.E., B.Tech, and B.Arch programs across the state.

Unlike many other states, REAP does not conduct a separate entrance examination. Instead, admissions are granted based on merit lists prepared from JEE Main scores, Class 12th percentages, or Diploma marks. The process typically involves multiple rounds of counseling to fill seats in government-aided and private technical institutions throughout Rajasthan.

Eligibility Requirements

B.E. / B.Tech Admissions

  • Academic Qualification: Candidates must have passed the Class 10+2 examination with Physics and Mathematics as mandatory subjects.
  • Minimum Marks: At least 45% aggregate marks in the qualifying examination (40% for reserved categories: SC/ST/PWD/OBC/SBC of Rajasthan).
  • Appearing Candidates: Students awaiting their final 12th-grade results are also eligible to apply provisionally.

B.Arch. Admissions

  • Academic Qualification: Passed 10+2 scheme of examination with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics OR passed 10+3 Diploma Examination with Mathematics as a compulsory subject.
  • Minimum Marks: At least 50% aggregate marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics and also at least 50% marks in aggregate of the 10+2 examination.
  • Entrance Exam: A valid NATA score is mandatory for admission.
